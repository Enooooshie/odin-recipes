Eggs

Your favorite classic scrambled eggs done right! A simple yet elegant dish requiring minimal effort with maximal results.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tsp mayonnaise or salad dressing
  • 1 tsp water (Optional)
  • 1 tsp margarine or butter
  • 1 pinch salt and pepper to taste
  1. In a cup or small bowl, whisk together the eggs, mayonnaise and water using a fork.
  2. Melt Margarine in a skillet over low heat
  3. Pour in the eggs, and stir constantly as they cook.
  4. Remove the eggs to a plate when they are set, but still moist.
  5. Do not over cook.
  6. Never add salt or pepper until eggs are on a plate, but these are also good without.
